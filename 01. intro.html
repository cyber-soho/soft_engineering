The Nature of Software

Unlike physical hardware that wears out over time, software deteriorates due to the impact of change. Each modification, fix, or feature addition can introduce new defects, causing the system's reliability to degrade. This interactive chart visualizes the difference.

The chart shows a comparison between the typical failure rate of hardware (the "bathtub curve") and the deterioration of software over its lifecycle as changes are introduced.

The Layers of Engineering
